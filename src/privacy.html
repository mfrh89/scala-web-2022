Collection and storage of personal data

Type and purpose of use

If you only use our website for informational purposes, i.e. you do not transmit information to us, we only collect the personal data that the browser of your end device automatically transmits to our server. This information is temporarily stored in a so-called log file. The following information is collected without your intervention and stored until automated deletion:

  • IP address
  • Date and time of the request
  • Time zone difference to Greenwich Mean Time (GMT)
  • Content of the request (specific page)
  • Access status/HTTP status code
  • Amount of data transferred in each case
  • Website from which the request comes
  • Browser
  • Operating system and its interface
  • Language and version of the browser software.

The aforementioned data is processed by us for the following purposes:

  • Ensuring a smooth connection setup of the website,
  • Ensuring a comfortable use of our website,
  • evaluation of system security and stability as well as
  • for further administrative purposes and for
  • abuse detection and troubleshooting

The legal basis for data processing is Art. 6 para. 1 p. 1 lit. f) DSGVO. Our legitimate interest follows from the purposes for data collection listed above. In no case do we use the collected data for the purpose of drawing conclusions about your person. In addition, we use cookies as well as analysis services when visiting our website, which are explained below.

Cookiebot (CMP)

For easy and transparent administration of consents required by data protection law, we use the Cookiebot Consent Management Platform (Cookiebit CMP) Cybot A/S, Havnegade 39, 1058 Copenhagen, Denmark. Cookiebot collects log file data and consent data using JavaScript. This JavaScript enables us to inform users about their consent to certain cookies and other technologies on our website and to obtain, manage and document such consent. The purpose is to comply with legal obligations and to store the consent for verification purposes. The legal basis for the processing of the data is Art. 6 para. 1 lit. c) DSGVO. The data is processed in the European Union. The consent data (consent and withdrawal of consent) will be stored for three years. If the purpose of storage for the specified processing purposes ceases to apply or the legally relevant storage period expires, the personal data will be routinely blocked or deleted in accordance with the statutory provisions. The criterion for the storage period is the relevant statutory retention period. You can permanently prevent the execution of JavaScript at any time by making the appropriate settings in your browser, which would also prevent Cookiebot from executing the JavaScript. Further information on data protection at Cookiebot: https://www.cookiebot.com/de/privacy-policy/ , Daniel Johannsen, Cybot A/S, at dpo@cookiebot.com.

Access to your consent settings

You have the option at any time to change your decision made via our Consent Management Platform and to subsequently grant or revoke your consents. You can find more information on data protection with regard to our cookies under data protection settings and you can access the setting options here: "Data protection settings".
